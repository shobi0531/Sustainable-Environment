DEFORESTATION

Deforestation is the process of clearing or removing forests or trees, often for the purpose of agricultural expansion, urban development, or logging. The consequences of deforestation include:

  • Loss of biodiversity
  • Disruption of ecosystems
  • Climate change due to reduced carbon sequestration
  • Soil erosion and degradation
  • Impact on indigenous communities

Efforts to combat deforestation include reforestation, sustainable logging practices, and conservation initiatives.
