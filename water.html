WATER POLLUTION

Water pollution is the contamination of water bodies, such as rivers, lakes, oceans, and groundwater, with harmful substances. Common water pollutants include:

  • Chemical pollutants
  • Biological pollutants
  • Suspended solids
  • Thermal pollution
  • Nutrient pollution
  • Heavy metals

Water pollution can have detrimental effects on aquatic ecosystems, drinking water quality, and public health.
