CLIMATE CHANGE

Climate change refers to long-term shifts in global temperatures and weather patterns. It is primarily driven by human activities, including the burning of fossil fuels and deforestation. Climate change can lead to a wide range of impacts, including:

  • Rising global temperatures
  • Sea-level rise
  • More frequent and severe weather events
  • Disruption of ecosystems
  • Threats to biodiversity
  • Impact on agriculture and food security

Addressing climate change is a critical global challenge that requires collective action and sustainable practices.
